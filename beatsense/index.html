What is BeatSense?

BeatSense is a Python-based educational system that demonstrates ECG signal preprocessing, feature extraction and rule-based arrhythmia classification. Use the demo to explore pre-computed MIT-BIH records or upload your own ECG (.mat/.csv/.txt) for a simulated analysis.
